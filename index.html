Know About Us

Rethinking how the world uses space.

POSOMEPA transforms unused spaces into opportunities—giving you instant access to places designed for work, creativity, and connection. From short meetings to special occasions, we ensure you find the perfect setting—exactly when you need it.

🎯
Our Mission
Make space accessible, flexible, and affordable by enabling seamless hourly bookings across a wide variety of environments.
🌍
Our Vision
Become a global leader in on-demand space rentals—redefining how individuals and businesses work, create, celebrate, and connect.

Know Your Founder

The mind behind
POSOMEPA.

Lokesh Chaturvedi
Founder & CEO, POSOMEPA
"Every space has potential. Every idle hour is an opportunity. POSOMEPA gives both a purpose."

With a background in architecture, Lokesh has been closely involved in the journey of creating spaces—from concept to completion. Along the way, he noticed a recurring pattern: spaces with immense potential often sitting idle.

That insight became the foundation of POSOMEPA—a platform designed to give every space a purpose, whenever it's needed. Driven by a belief that accessibility and flexibility should define how we interact with the world around us, Lokesh built POSOMEPA to bridge the gap between unused spaces and the people who need them.

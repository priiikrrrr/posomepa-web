About Us

Every great moment deserves the right space.

POSOMEPA connects space owners with people who need them — turning idle hours into income, and every search into the perfect match. Whether you're hosting a spare room or booking a studio for the afternoon, we make it instant, flexible, and completely hassle-free.

🎯
Our Mission
Make space accessible, flexible, and affordable — with seamless hourly bookings across every kind of environment imaginable.
🌍
Our Vision
Become the world's go-to platform for on-demand space — redefining how people work, create, celebrate, and connect.
How it works

Your perfect space, booked in three easy steps.

From discovery to confirmation — the entire flow takes under a minute.

Know Your Founder

The mind behind
POSOMEPA.

Lokesh Chaturvedi
Founder & CEO, POSOMEPA
"Every space has potential. Every idle hour, an opportunity. POSOMEPA gives both a purpose."

With a background in architecture, Lokesh spent years designing and delivering spaces — from first sketches to finished rooms. Along the way, he noticed a recurring pattern: spaces with immense potential, sitting idle.

That insight became the foundation of POSOMEPA — a platform built on the belief that accessibility and flexibility should define how we interact with the world around us. POSOMEPA ensures no great space ever goes to waste — and no one ever settles for the wrong one.
